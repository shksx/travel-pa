# Travel Personal Assistant — Project Vision

## Overview

A proactive, intelligent travel personal assistant capable of researching, planning, and managing all aspects of travel — going well beyond the capabilities of free AI tools by combining account access, calendar awareness, real-time data, and deeply personalized recommendations.

---

## Core Research & Booking Capabilities

The assistant handles research and comparison across:

- Accommodation (hotels, Airbnb, house-sitting, etc.)
- Airfares
- Car hire
- General transportation (trains, ferries, buses, rideshare)
- Local restaurants (filtered by dietary requirements)
- Grocery options (filtered by dietary requirements)
- Things to do (based on personal preferences and interests)
- Foreign exchange rates across providers
- Travel insurance

---

## Proactive Intelligence

The assistant doesn't wait to be asked — it monitors and acts ahead of time:

- Views the user's calendar to identify upcoming trips with no accommodation booked and proactively asks if it should start searching based on destination
- Detects booking clashes as they're made and alerts the user
- Monitors flight bookings and researches visa requirements, airport transfer options, and useful local apps for the destination
- Understands context from user history and tailors suggestions to known budget preferences
- Periodically reviews new travel platforms and accommodation providers based on peer reviews (e.g., "We've noticed Vio is offering better prices for your upcoming destinations — we'll incorporate it into searches and flag loyalty point differences")

**Example of smart, context-aware alerting:**
> "You're landing at Osaka airport at 9:30pm. Trains can stop running after 10:20pm so if you are late, here are your options. History shows around 15% of these flights arrive too late for the train. I can leave a booking link handy if you need it."

---

## Travel Planning Features

- **Visual timeline and map** of the upcoming trip, shareable with others
- **Day trip direction optimization** — recommends the best order of travel to minimize time, maximize enjoyment, and avoid crowds
- **Real-time traffic and delay alerts** — knowing planned destinations, alerts to hazards and suggests alternatives
- **Flight status monitoring** — automatic alerts for delays, gate changes, and other relevant updates
- **Weather updates and packing reminders** (e.g., "Rain is predicted for the first week. Do you require a jacket or poncho?")
- **Safe street tracking** — location-aware safety alerts (e.g., "Steer clear of this area")

---

## Document & Identity Management

Access to a pre-arranged document file containing:

- Travel insurance details
- Boarding passes
- Photo ID and passport (with renewal reminders)
- Visa and ESTA arrangements
- Passport restriction and expiry monitoring
- Vaccination suggestions for destinations
- Auto-fill forms from stored personal information

---

## Financial Integration

- Syncs with travel budgeting apps to report under/overspending
- Optionally creates its own budget directly from bank account details
- Monitors and compares foreign exchange rates across providers
- Flags loyalty point differences between booking platforms

---

## Communication & Sharing

- Email integration to pull travel-relevant data automatically
- Share trip information (itinerary, timeline, map) with travel companions or family
- Supports both solo travel and couples/groups — asks upfront whether the trip is solo or shared so both parties' needs are met (with awareness that couples sometimes travel separately)

---

## Nudges & Reminders

Timely, context-aware nudges sent to the user's preferred devices including watch:

- "Check-in is two hours away"
- Packing reminders tied to weather forecasts
- Passport/document expiry warnings
- Pre-departure safety and logistics briefings

---

## Post-Trip Debrief

After returning, the assistant offers:

- A budget summary comparing planned vs. actual spend
- Offer to organize a photo book or journey montage
- Trip review and notes for future reference

---

## Interface & Accessibility

- **Multi-device** — functions across phone, tablet, desktop, and watch
- **One-click screen** within a personalized app
- **Voice mode** for hands-free interaction
- Watch integration for reminders and updates

---

## Technical Ambitions

- Ability to log in to user accounts across a range of booking and travel websites on the user's behalf
- Location awareness for real-time routing and safety
- Calendar integration (read access)
- Email integration (read access for travel data extraction)
- Bank/budget app integration
- Peer review monitoring for platform and provider quality
